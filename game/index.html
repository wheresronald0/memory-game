Instructions for the game

Concentraion, also known as Match Match, Memeory, Pelmanism,Pexesco or simply Pairs; is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface adn tow card are flipped face up over each turn, The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
